IITK Samanvay is an initiative that brings together visionaries from industry and academia. Created to bridge the gap between research excellence and real-world application, it enables meaningful dialogue, sparks strategic partnerships, and drives action towards a future shaped by innovation.
Set to take place on 2nd & 3rd September 2025, this year’s edition is themed “Igniting Ideas. Accelerating Innovation.” The event will spotlight collaborative growth and pioneering possibilities across the evolving landscape of innovation, with a strong focus on Artificial Intelligence (AI), Sustainability, and Cybersecurity — the three key pillars driving transformative change around the world today.
